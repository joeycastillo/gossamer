void i2c_init ( void  )

Initializes the I2C peripheral.

Initializes the peripheral with a bus speed of 400kHz. You are still responsible for setting the SDA and SCL pins to the correct peripheral pin mux.

Note
Requires that your board have I2C_SERCOM_APBCMASK and I2C_GCLK_CLKCTRL_ID defined.
